Bio

My name is Jeffrey Rivera Negron. I am currently in the Game Design program at UCF as my childhood was littered with video games. Outside of that, I love stories and music, whether that is performing or trying to experiment and write my own. I came to UCF because it was the only school that had its own uniqueness that set them apart from the other schools in Florida.


My Work

My goal is to eventually join a game studio that is very knowledgeable in story-based games and gain a position there where I can be in charge of my own game, with my own written story and lore. But as long as I end up working on games that I believe are worth being created, then that’s where I’ll start.
